break

Break will break the loop at the give condition. 

                        for (let counter = 1; counter<=10; counter++){
                            if( conter == 5){
                                break
                            }
                            document.write(counter)
                            document.write('
')
                        }
                        output
                        1
                        2
                        3
                        4

Continue

Continue will break the loop at satisfied condition and the continue further. 

                        for (let counter = 1; counter<=10; counter++){
                            if( counter == 5){
                                continue
                            }
                            document.write(counter)
                            document.write('
')
                        }
                        output
                        1
                        2
                        3
                        4
                        6
                        7
                        8
                        9
                        10
